About

Quick Quiz is a simple webapp that uses Python and Flask. It runs in a local server on your computer.

Usage

On the homepage, insert the terms you want to study in the textbox, with one term per line. Click "Start" to begin the quiz.

While taking the quiz, check your answer for each term using a popup dictionary, then click either pass or fail. To pass or fail terms, you can also use the shortcuts 'space' or 'z' respectively.

The quiz ends when each of the terms have been passed once.
